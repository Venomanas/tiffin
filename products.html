Greek salad

A traditional Greek salad, also known as "Horiatiki," is a refreshing and vibrant dish featuring crisp cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, tangy red onions, briny Kalamata olives, and creamy feta cheese, all drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and sprinkled with dried oregano. It's a delightful combination of flavors and textures that makes it a perfect appetizer or side dish for any meal.

Poha

Poha is a traditional Indian breakfast dish made from flattened rice flakes, cooked with onions, mustard seeds, and spices. It's light, flavorful, and commonly garnished with fresh cilantro and lemon juice.

Millets roti

Millets roti is a type of Indian flatbread made with millet flour instead of the usual wheat flour. It's a healthier alternative and has a slightly nutty flavor. The dough is rolled out into thin circles and then cooked on a griddle until golden brown. It's a nutritious and tasty option to enjoy with curries or chutneys. Give it a try for a wholesome meal!

Methi oats roti

Methi oats roti is a nutritious flatbread made with a combination of oats, fenugreek leaves (methi), and spices. The oats add a healthy twist while the methi brings a unique flavor. The dough is rolled out and cooked on a griddle until crispy. It's a wholesome and tasty option, perfect for a healthy meal! Give it a go for a delicious and nutritious treat!
